About

Who I am and what I do

I'm Lu3ky13 (Ahmad A Abdulla) — a penetration tester, bug bounty hunter, and co-founder of CyberShield and @suly_con. Based in Kurdistan, I hunt vulnerabilities on HackerOne and help organizations harden their attack surface.

My work spans web application security (SQL injection, XSS, CSRF), API security (IDOR/BOLA, SSRF), cloud and endpoint agent research, memory forensics, and red team operations. I've been recognized on HackerOne's reputation leaderboard during 2022–2023.

I share techniques on X and Medium, and teach through cybershield.krd and CyberShield CTF platforms.
